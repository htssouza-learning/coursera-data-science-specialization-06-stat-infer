Statistical Inference: Simulating Exponential Distributions

Henrique Souza (github.com/htssouza)

Overview

This report investigates the exponential distributions in R and compares them with the Central Limit Theorem (CLT).

Simulations

Defining our random seed and loading libraries.

set.seed(123456)
library(ggplot2)

Creating 1000 samples of 40 exponential each. For all the simulations lambda will be equal to 0.2.

lambda <- 0.2
sample.size <- 40
sample.count <- 1000

Now lets generate our simulation data.

sample.data <- matrix(rexp(sample.size * sample.count, lambda), sample.count, sample.size)

Summary of our data:

str(sample.data)
##  num [1:1000, 1:40] 2.98 2.54 6.29 5.3 5.69 ...
